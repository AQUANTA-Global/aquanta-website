Frontiers of Discovery

AQUANTA explores water molecular networks as early reporters of system state in medicine, biology, agriculture and the environment.

Research Directions

Regenerative medicine, cancer, neuroscience, aging, agriculture and environmental monitoring are approached through a shared question: can water spectral patterns reveal system-level change before conventional signs appear?

Method

Aquaphotomics combines spectroscopy, perturbation, multivariate modeling and Aquagram® interpretation to connect water organization with function, adaptation and resilience.
